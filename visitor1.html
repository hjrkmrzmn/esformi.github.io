    About Us


    DEDICATED TO SAVING LIFE.

    We provide a blood and transplantation service to the Ministery of Health, looking after blood donation services in Malaysia and transplant services across the counrty. This includes managing the donation, storage and transplantation of blood.

    • To spread awareness of the importance of blood donation
    • Optimise the safety ands supply of blood

    • Provide expert advice to the community about blood donation
