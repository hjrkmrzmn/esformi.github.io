MENTARI MALAYSIA

MENTARI is a new approach initiated by the Malaysian Ministry of Health (MOH) to increase outreach and reintegration of patients with mental health problems into the community. For your information, there is at least one MENTARI in each state managed by Department Psychiatry and Mental Health.

Address: MENTARI HQ@Selayang

LG Lot 25B,26,27 Selayang Capitol Kompleks

Selayang – Kepong Highway,

68100 Kuala Lumpur

Tel Number: 03-6127 0946

The objective of MIASA is to promote awareness on the importance of mental health, to address and clarify misconceptions on mental health issues and conditions while simultaneously provide support for peers and caregivers through its various programmes and support initiatives.
